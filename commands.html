Commands

NovaLearn uses natural AI conversations instead of traditional command-based interactions. Simply mention the bot or reply to one of its messages and ask your question naturally.

Invite Bot Support Server

💬 Natural Conversation Examples

  • @NovaLearn Explain Newton's Laws.
  • @NovaLearn Solve x² + 5x + 6 = 0.
  • @NovaLearn Write an English essay on Climate Change.
  • @NovaLearn Explain Surah Al-Fatihah.
  • @NovaLearn Help me prepare for tomorrow's Biology exam.

⚙️ Available Commands

/help
General

Displays the complete help menu, bot information, useful links, and how to interact with NovaLearn.

/invite
General

Returns the official invite link so you can add NovaLearn to your Discord server.

/support
General

Provides the official Discord Support Server link.

/about
Information

Learn about NovaLearn, its mission, supported subjects, and educational goals.

/subjects
Education

Displays every supported subject and learning category available in NovaLearn.

/ping
Utility

Shows the bot's current response time and API latency.

/translate
Utility

Translate text between multiple languages while preserving meaning and context.

/math
Mathematics

Solve equations, algebra, geometry, calculus, trigonometry, statistics, probability, and word problems with step-by-step explanations.

/physics
Science

Ask physics questions covering mechanics, optics, electricity, magnetism, thermodynamics, waves, and modern physics.

/chemistry
Science

Get help with organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, reactions, equations, balancing, periodic table, and laboratory concepts.

/biology
Science

Learn genetics, anatomy, ecology, botany, zoology, microbiology, biotechnology, and human biology.

/english
Languages

Improve grammar, essays, vocabulary, comprehension, reading, writing, and speaking.

/urdu
Languages

Receive assistance with Urdu grammar, literature, essays, poetry, and translations.

/programming
Computer Science

Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Java, C++, SQL, React, Node.js, Git, APIs, and software development.

/history
Humanities

Explore world history, Islamic history, civilizations, wars, revolutions, and historical events.

/geography
Humanities

Learn maps, countries, climate, oceans, population, agriculture, and natural resources.

/economics
Business

Study microeconomics, macroeconomics, banking, finance, business, and trade.

/politics
Social Studies

Understand constitutions, governments, democracy, public administration, and international relations.

/islamic
Islamic Studies

Learn about the Quran, Hadith, Seerah, Islamic history, ethics, and Islamic knowledge.

/grammar
Writing

Correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, and sentence structure.

/essay
Writing

Generate well-structured essays, reports, assignments, and academic writing.

/summarize
AI Tools

Summarize articles, notes, research papers, and long documents into concise explanations.

/quiz
Education

Generate quizzes and practice questions for any subject.

/exam
Education

Create revision plans, mock exams, summaries, and exam preparation material.

/feedback
Support

Submit suggestions, bug reports, or feature requests to improve NovaLearn.
